The Story of Remote Class

Imagine sitting in a classroom. You are working a math problem and they are starting to get tough. Luckily your teacher is just a short hand raise away. Within a few moments they are at your desk helping you understand where you went wrong and what you need to know to solve the next problem.

Unfortunately this is incredibly hard to do online. Teachers are forced to try and fit tool like Skype and Chat applications to keep that same type of classroom interaction.

Remote Class is a single solution for teachers to have the interactive learning expereince of a classroom, online.
